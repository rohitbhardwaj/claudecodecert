# Easy Questions

## Q1. Why should Claude not implement immediately?

## Answer

Because partial refunds affect money and production behavior.

## Reasoning

The team must agree on rules, risks, tests, owners, and rollback first.

---

## Q2. What is an approval checkpoint?

## Answer

A written plan that shows what Claude will change and what risks were considered.

## Reasoning

Approval should be based on evidence, not guesswork.

---

## Q3. Who should approve refund calculation?

## Answer

Finance/payments and architecture owners.

## Reasoning

Refund calculations affect customer money and business policy.

---

## Q4. Why include rollback?

## Answer

The team needs a safe way to recover if partial refunds fail.

## Reasoning

Production behavior changes can create incidents.
