# Scoring Rubric

## 5 — Excellent

Participant defines all approval evidence and maps each area to the correct owner.

## 4 — Strong

Participant covers most areas but misses one failure path or owner.

## 3 — Acceptable

Participant covers behavior and tests but misses rollback, data, or logging.

## 2 — Weak

Participant treats approval as informal and lacks evidence.

## 1 — Poor

Participant approves Claude to implement based only on happy path.
