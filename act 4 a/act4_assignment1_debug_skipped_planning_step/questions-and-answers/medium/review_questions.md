# Medium Questions

## Q1. Why should pricing/finance owners be involved?

## Answer

Coupon stacking affects money behavior.

## Reasoning

Discount calculation impacts customer totals, refunds, and possibly financial reporting.

---

## Q2. Why is rewriting a failing pricing test risky?

## Answer

It may hide a real defect.

## Reasoning

A failing test is evidence that behavior changed or a requirement was violated.

---

## Q3. Why inspect mobile checkout?

## Answer

Coupon behavior must be consistent across entry points.

## Reasoning

If only web is tested, mobile may return a different total.

---

## Q4. Why inspect the API contract?

## Answer

Coupon stacking may change response fields or totals.

## Reasoning

API changes require owner approval and client compatibility review.
