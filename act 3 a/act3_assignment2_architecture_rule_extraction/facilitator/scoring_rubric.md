# Scoring Rubric

## 5 — Excellent

Participant extracts all key rules, places them in the correct repo artifacts, and writes specific `CLAUDE.md` guidance.

## 4 — Strong

Participant extracts most rules but misses one placement or owner.

## 3 — Acceptable

Participant identifies messy patterns but writes some vague rules.

## 2 — Weak

Participant finds issues but does not convert them into repo-readable rules.

## 1 — Poor

Participant leaves architecture knowledge as informal review comments.
